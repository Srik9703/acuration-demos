Shruti Sain
Shruti Sain
Chief Financial Officer, STS
Shruti is a pioneering CFO in the sustainable aviation fuel market.
To Shruti, being a Futureshaper means driving a sense of purpose and meaning in everything she does. One of her most fulfilling projects at Honeywell was the investment in a new clean tech venture with Alder Fuels – powering the largest sustainable fuel agreement in aviation history. From the complex financial modeling to the final agreement, Shruti in collaboration with her team reached a historic milestone: one step closer to supporting the growth of a zero-carbon economy.
