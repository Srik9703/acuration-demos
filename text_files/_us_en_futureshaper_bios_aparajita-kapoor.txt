Aparajita Kapoor
General Manager
Aparajita is fueling innovation with high-quality sustainable solutions.
Aparajita learned the value of resources like potable water, and clean energy and electricity growing up in India where they weren’t always accessible. As someone who has struggled with asthma, working on clean air technologies is especially important to her. Aparajita works on multiple innovative materials that are making a real difference, from Spectra Shield® products used in advanced armor applications, to Solstice® refrigerants, she’s championing technology that’s helping to reduce our carbon footprint.
